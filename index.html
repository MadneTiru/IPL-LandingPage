Royal Challengers Bangaluru

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a professional cricket franchise based in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. They compete in the Indian Premier League (IPL), one of the most prestigious Twenty20 cricket leagues globally. Established in 2008, RCB has garnered a massive fan following over the years, known for their vibrant red and black attire and passionate supporters. The team has boasted star players like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Chris Gayle, among others, making them perennial contenders in the IPL.

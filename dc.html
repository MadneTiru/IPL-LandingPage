Delhi Capitals

Delhi Capitals (formerly known as Delhi Daredevils) is a professional cricket franchise based in Delhi, India, competing in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Established in 2008, the team underwent a rebranding in 2018, adopting the name Delhi Capitals to reflect a fresh identity and renewed vigor. The franchise has showcased a mix of seasoned veterans and emerging talents, aiming for consistent success in the IPL. Led by dynamic leaders such as Shreyas Iyer and Rishabh Pant, Delhi Capitals have demonstrated their competitive edge, consistently challenging the top teams in the league. With a loyal fanbase backing them, Delhi Capitals continue to strive for their maiden IPL title, epitomizing the spirit of cricket in the capital city of India.

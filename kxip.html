Kings XI Punjab

Kings XI Punjab (KXIP) is a cricket franchise that competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is based in Mohali, Punjab, and represents the state of Punjab in India. Established in 2008 as one of the original eight franchises, KXIP has had a mixed history in the IPL. The team has boasted notable players like Yuvraj Singh, Chris Gayle, and KL Rahul, among others. Despite consistently fielding competitive teams, KXIP has yet to clinch an IPL title.

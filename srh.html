Sunriser's Hyderabad

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is a professional cricket franchise based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, and competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2012, SRH quickly established itself as a formidable team in the league. Known for its orange jersey, representing the colors of the rising sun, SRH has garnered a strong following among cricket enthusiasts. The franchise has boasted a talented roster of players, including international stars like David Warner, Kane Williamson, and Rashid Khan, who have played pivotal roles in SRH's success over the years. Under the astute leadership of captains like Warner and Williamson, SRH has clinched the IPL title once in 2016 and has consistently been a playoff contender.
